About Mesrop Arcade

Mesrop Arcade was created to offer quick and engaging entertainment to anyone with a web browser. Our mini games are designed to be lightweight, fast and fun on both desktop and mobile devices. We believe in keeping things simple—no unnecessary sign‑ups or downloads, just games you can play instantly.

Whether you're taking a short break at work or looking for a casual challenge, Mesrop Arcade has something for you. All of our games are built using modern, open‑source technologies and are completely free to play.

We are committed to improving and expanding the collection. If you have feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out via the contact page.
